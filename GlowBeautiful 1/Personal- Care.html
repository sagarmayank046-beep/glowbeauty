Daily Personal Care – How to Use & What to Choose

Personal care routine sahi order me follow karna bahut zaroori hai. Neeche humne simple language me bataya hai ki kaunsa product pehle use kare aur kaunsa baad me.

Step-by-Step Personal Care Routine

  1. Face Wash / Cleanser – Sabse pehle face ko clean kare taaki dirt & oil remove ho.
  2. Toner (Optional) – Pores tighten karta hai aur skin ko next step ke liye ready karta hai.
  3. Serum – Acne, dark spots, hair fall jaise problems ke liye targeted treatment.
  4. Moisturizer – Skin ko hydrate aur repair karta hai (oily skin bhi skip na kare).
  5. Sunscreen (Morning me) – Sun damage, pigmentation aur aging se protection.

🌞 Morning Routine

Face Wash → Serum → Moisturizer → Sunscreen

🌙 Night Routine

Face Wash → Treatment Serum → Moisturizer
